Tips Packing for 7 Days Carry-On Only
  • Rule of 33 shirts, 3 underwear, 3 socks — mix and match, hand-wash as needed. Hotels and Airbnbs both have sinks.
  • ShoesWear your bulkiest shoes on travel days — they take the most space in a bag. Limit to 2 pairs max.
  • ToiletriesBuy full-size toiletries on arrival at a London Boots or Paris pharmacy — cheaper than TSA-compliant travel sizes and frees up bag spaceBoots is everywhere in London
  • SouvenirsBudget space or weight for souvenirs on the return — if buying books or manga at Forbidden Planet or Aaapoum Bapoum, factor that in before you leave homeManga shops = heavy books
  • Personal itemUse your personal item (backpack) as your day bag throughout the trip — keeps your carry-on in the overhead and your daily essentials always on you
